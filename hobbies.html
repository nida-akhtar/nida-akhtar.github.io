04 / Off the keyboard

Life beyond the terminal.

The things I do when I'm not writing code or training models. They keep me grounded, and I think they make me a better engineer too.

Calligraphy

I've been practicing calligraphy for some time, all self-taught. It takes patience and a steady hand, and getting better at it has been one of the more rewarding things I've done outside of academics.

I share my work on Instagram at @calligraphy_on_page if you'd like to take a look.

Cooking

I love experimenting with recipes and adding my own spin to them, and I'm happy to say it usually works out.

Cats

I'm a cat person. I love their quiet ways, their little habits, and how they manage to make a room feel calmer just by being in it.

Tea, and good conversation

Tea, for me, is less of a daily habit and more of a cultural one. Tea is rarely just tea; it's "let's discuss this over chai," "come for evening tea," "you're doing nothing? let's have tea." It's the reason for sitting down together more than anything else.

Sports & community

I enjoy badminton and cricket: both for the games themselves and for the people you end up playing with. I also genuinely enjoy organizing events: the planning, the logistics, the small satisfaction of seeing something you put together actually come to life.
